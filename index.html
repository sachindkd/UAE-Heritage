UAE History Timeline

1971

Formation of the UAE

The United Arab Emirates was officially formed on December 2, 1971, uniting seven emirates into one country.

2009

UAE Mars Mission Announcement

The UAE announced plans to launch its first mission to Mars, paving the way for the country's space exploration ambitions.

2010

Burj Khalifa Completion

The world's tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, was completed in Dubai, showcasing the UAE's architectural innovation.

2020

Hope Probe Mission to Mars

The UAE successfully launched its Hope Probe, the first interplanetary mission by an Arab country, to Mars.

2021

Golden Jubilee of the UAE

The UAE celebrated its 50th anniversary, marking half a century of remarkable growth and achievements.
